## ğŸ¨ Recording Tips

### **Screen Recording Settings:**
- Resolution: 1920x1080 minimum
- Frame rate: 30fps or 60fps
- Clean desktop (hide icons, close unnecessary apps)
- Use demo content (not real client work)

- **Free:** DaVinci Resolve, CapCut
- **Paid:** Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro

- **macOS:** QuickTime (built-in) or Screen Studio
- **Windows:** OBS Studio, ShareX
- **Cross-platform:** OBS Studio
